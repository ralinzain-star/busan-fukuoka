## 5. Best Seasons to Visit Busan

### Season Classification

```
PEAK SEASON (most crowded, highest prices):
  - July-August: Beach season, summer holidays, Busan Sea Festival
  - October: BIFF, Jagalchi Festival, perfect autumn weather, foliage

HIGH SEASON:
  - April-May: Cherry blossoms, pleasant weather, many festivals
  - September: Post-summer, Chuseok, warm but comfortable

SHOULDER SEASON (good balance of weather + value):
  - March: Cherry blossom start, still cool, fewer crowds
  - November: Late autumn, mild weather, post-festival calm
  - December (early): Winter light festivals beginning, budget-friendly

OFF-SEASON / LOW SEASON (cheapest, fewest tourists):
  - January-February: Cold (3-7°C), fewest tourists, cheapest flights/hotels
  - June: Monsoon/rainy season (jangma), high humidity
```

**Best overall months:** May and October

**Avoid if possible:** Late June-mid July (heaviest monsoon rains)
